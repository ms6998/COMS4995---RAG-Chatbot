MS Computer Science - Degree Requirements (2023 Catalog)

Program Overview:
The Master of Science in Computer Science requires a minimum of 30 credits of graduate-level coursework. The program is designed to provide advanced knowledge in computer science fundamentals and specialized areas.

Core Requirements (12 credits):
Students must complete FOUR core courses (12 credits total) from the following areas:

1. Systems Track:
   - COMS 4111 Introduction to Databases (3 credits)
   - COMS 4115 Programming Languages and Translators (3 credits)
   - COMS 4118 Operating Systems (3 credits)

2. Theory and Algorithms:
   - COMS 4231 Analysis of Algorithms (3 credits)
   - COMS 4236 Computational Complexity (3 credits)

3. Artificial Intelligence and Applications:
   - COMS 4701 Artificial Intelligence (3 credits)
   - COMS 4771 Machine Learning (3 credits)

4. Software Engineering:
   - COMS 4156 Advanced Software Engineering (3 credits)
   - COMS 4995 Software Development Project (3 credits)

Students must select at least ONE course from each of THREE different areas listed above.

Technical Electives (15 credits):
Students must complete 15 credits of technical electives. These electives must be:
- At least 3 credits must be from 6000-level COMS courses
- Can include approved courses from COMS, IEOR, ELEN, MATH departments
- Must be approved by the academic advisor

Approved 6000-level courses include:
- COMS 6111 Advanced Database Systems
- COMS 6156 Topics in Software Engineering
- COMS 6701 Advanced Machine Learning
- COMS 6998 Topics in Computer Science

Track Options:
Students can optionally follow a specialized track:

Machine Learning Track:
Required:
- COMS 4771 Machine Learning (core requirement)
- COMS 4772 Advanced Machine Learning
- COMS 4774 Unsupervised Learning
Plus 2 additional ML-related electives

Natural Language Processing Track:
Required:
- COMS 4705 Natural Language Processing
- COMS 4995 Deep Learning for NLP
Plus 2 additional NLP-related electives

Computer Systems Track:
Required:
- COMS 4111 Databases (core)
- COMS 4118 Operating Systems (core)
- COMS 4119 Computer Networks
- COMS 4156 Advanced Software Engineering (core)

Thesis Option (3 credits):
Students may substitute 3 credits of electives with:
- COMS 6999 Master's Thesis (3 credits)
Requires advisor approval and thesis defense.

Prerequisites:
Students are expected to have undergraduate-level knowledge in:
- Data Structures and Algorithms
- Discrete Mathematics
- Computer Organization
- At least one programming language (Java, C++, or Python)

Deficiency courses may be required if prerequisites are not met.

GPA Requirements:
- Minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 required for graduation
- No more than two grades below B- are permitted
- Grade of C+ or better required in core courses

Time Limit:
- Full-time students: Complete within 2 years
- Part-time students: Complete within 5 years

Course Load:
- Full-time: 9-12 credits per semester
- Part-time: 3-8 credits per semester

Transfer Credits:
- Maximum 6 credits from other institutions may be transferred
- Courses must be graduate-level with grade B or better
- Must be approved by department

Additional Requirements:
- Academic Integrity: All students must complete academic integrity training
- Enrollment: Must maintain continuous enrollment (excluding summers)
- Residency: At least 24 credits must be completed at Columbia
