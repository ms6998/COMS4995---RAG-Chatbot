TITLE: BARNARD-COLUMBIA-CHAMBR SINGER
SUBJECT: Music
INSTRUCTOR: Gail R Archer
DESCRIPTION: Prerequisites: contact Barnard College, Department of Music (854-5096). Membership in the chorus is open to all men and women in the University community. The chorus gives several public concerts each season, both on and off campus, often with other performing organizations. Sight-singing sessions offered. The repertory includes works from all periods of music literature.
