TITLE: CULTR,MOTIVATN,PROSOCIAL BEHAV
SUBJECT: Psychology
INSTRUCTOR: Svetlana Komissarouk
DESCRIPTION: Prerequisites: Some knowledge of Research Methods, Statistics, and Social Psychology, plus Instructors Permission. Reviews and integrates current research on three important topics of social psychology: culture, motivation, and prosocial behavior. Discussions and readings will cover theoretical principles, methodological approaches, and the intersection of these three topics. Students will write a personal research proposal based on the theories presented during the seminar.
