TITLE: Advanced Health Assessment, Pathophysiol
SUBJECT: Nursing
INSTRUCTOR: Shanelly Singh
DESCRIPTION: Advanced Health Assessment, Pathophysiology, and Pharmacology provides students with the advanced knowledge and competencies essential to enhance clinical decision-making in complex healthcare environments, across the health-illness continuum, and across the lifespan. Emphasis will be placed on examining common etiologies, pathogenetic mechanisms, clinical manifestations, treatments, and pharmacologic interventions to enhance a holistic approach to patient care.
