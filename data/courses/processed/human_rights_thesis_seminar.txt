TITLE: HUMAN RIGHTS THESIS SEMINAR
SUBJECT: Human Rights
INSTRUCTOR: Tracey Holland
DESCRIPTION: Prerequisites: HRTS UN3994 Human Rights Senior Seminar: Research Methods. Additional information available at: http://humanrightscolumbia.org/education/undergraduate This course is designed for human rights students who wish to write a honors-eligible thesis. The course will consist of group sessions, during which time students will present their work and participate in discussions, and individual meetings with the thesis supervisor. The course instructor is the thesis supervisor for each student.
