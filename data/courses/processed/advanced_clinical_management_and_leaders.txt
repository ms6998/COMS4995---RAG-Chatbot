TITLE: Advanced Clinical Management and Leaders
SUBJECT: Nursing
INSTRUCTOR: Anita Volpe
DESCRIPTION: This graduate course will focus on health service systems.Students will study the theories, competencies, and concepts of management and leadership. Furthermore students will examine the leadership role related to quality and safety in complex health service delivery systems, the management theories and concepts such as interprofessional communication, teamwork, delegation and supervision.The core role competencies for the nurse leader frame the course activities.
