TITLE: DEVICE NANOFABRICATION
SUBJECT: Electrical Engineering
INSTRUCTOR: Jose A Bahamonde
DESCRIPTION: This course provides an understanding of the methods used for structuring matter on the nanometer length: thin-film technology; lithographic patterning and technologies including photon, electron, ion and atom, scanning probe, soft lithography, and nanoimprinting; pattern transfer; self-assembly; process integration; and applications.
