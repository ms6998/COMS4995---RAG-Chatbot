TITLE: FRENCH NEW WAVE CINEMA
SUBJECT: French
INSTRUCTOR: Anne Boyman
DESCRIPTION: This course traces the emergence and development of “New Wave” cinema in France in the 60s. Through a detailed analysis of some of its most iconic films: 400 Blows, Breathless, Hiroshima mon amour… the course will examine the radical artistic and social innovations of its major “auteurs”; Truffaut, Godard, Resnais et al. FREN BC1204: French Intermediate II or the equivalent level is required.
