TITLE: FUNDRAISING FOR LARGE INSTITUTIONS: EDs
SUBJECT: Nonprofit Management
INSTRUCTOR: Patrick O'Connell
DESCRIPTION: This course’s goal is to enable students to understand the specific challenges and particularities to medical and educational institutions’ fundraising, administration and community engagement. The successful student will complete the course with an extensive understanding of fundraising strategy, revenue streams, governance, organizational structure, leadership models, public policy, trends in the industry, and fundraising best practices as they relate to medical and educational organizations (“Eds and Meds”).
