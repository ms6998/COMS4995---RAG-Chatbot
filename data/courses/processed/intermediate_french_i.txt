TITLE: INTERMEDIATE FRENCH I
SUBJECT: French
INSTRUCTOR: Laurence Marie
DESCRIPTION: This course will further your awareness and understanding of the French language, culture and literature, provide a comprehensive review of fundamental grammar points while introducing more advanced ones, as well as improve your mastery of oral, reading, and writing skills. By the end of the course, you will be able to read short to medium-length literary and non-literary texts, and analyze and comment on varied documents and topics, both orally and in writing.
