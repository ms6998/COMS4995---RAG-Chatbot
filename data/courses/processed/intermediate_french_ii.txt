TITLE: INTERMEDIATE FRENCH II
SUBJECT: French
INSTRUCTOR: Heidi Holst-Knudsen
DESCRIPTION: Prerequisites: FREN UN2121 Intermediate Conversation is a suggested, not required, corequisite Prepares students for advanced French language and culture. Develops skills in speaking, reading, and writing French. Emphasizes cross-cultural awareness through the study of short stories, films, and passages from novels. Fosters the ability to write about and discuss a variety of topics using relatively complex structures.
