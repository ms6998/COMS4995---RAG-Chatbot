TITLE: NONPROFIT DEVELOPMENT, FUNDRAISING, AND
SUBJECT: Nonprofit Management
INSTRUCTOR: Ramone Segree
DESCRIPTION: This course provides a comprehensive overview of the design, implementation and management of the components of a philanthropic program and its relationship to the financial sustainability of the nonprofit organization. It introduces the philosophical, ethical and historical underpinnings of fundraising practice, also providing the nomenclature, characteristics, and methods of gift generation and their sources, and the management and stewardship of those sources. Additionally, it examines the relationship of the organization’s mission to its strategic vision and the planning, management and impact of fundraising to the organization’s advancement and sustainability.
