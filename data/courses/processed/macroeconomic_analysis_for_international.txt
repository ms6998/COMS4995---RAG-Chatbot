TITLE: Macroeconomic Analysis for International
SUBJECT: School Of Int & Public Affairs
INSTRUCTOR: Fernando Cirelli
DESCRIPTION: MPA and MIA-Track II Economics Core. This course continues the one-year sequence initiated with SIPA IA6400 and focuses on macroeconomics. The goal of this course is to provide students with the analytical framework to examine and interpret observed economic events in the global economy. The causal relationships between macroeconomic aggregates is based upon microeconomic principles. The subject matter always refers to concrete situations with a particular focus on the causes and effects of the current global financial crisis. The controversial nature of macroeconomic policies is central.
