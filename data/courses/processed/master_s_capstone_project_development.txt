TITLE: Master's Capstone Project Development
SUBJECT: Nursing
INSTRUCTOR: Kathleen Mullen
DESCRIPTION: This graduate course is designed to provide the student with the knowledge and skills to facilitate changes in practice delivery using quality improvement strategies. Historical development for total quality management and strategies for implementing process improvement are emphasized. Students will learn how to develop a culture of appreciative inquiry to foster inquisition and innovation. Upon completion of this course, students will design a plan for implementation of a quality improvement project.
