TITLE: INTERMEDIATE SPANISH II
SUBJECT: Spanish
INSTRUCTOR: Alfonso Cid
DESCRIPTION: Prerequisites: SPAN UN2101 or a score of 450-625 in the departments Placement Examination. An intensive course in Spanish language communicative competence, with stress on oral interaction, reading, writing and culture as a continuation of SPAN UN2101. All Columbia students must take Spanish language courses (UN 1101-3300) for a letter grade.
