TITLE: MOTIVATIONAL INTERVIEWING LAB
SUBJECT: Social Work
INSTRUCTOR: Shuronia N Johnson
DESCRIPTION: The skills-based lab is a 7-week “hands-on” training for all students enrolled in Foundation Year Field. The aim of the lab is to equip social work students with universal engagement and communication skills to support both professional development in the field and personal growth. The lab is designed to be highly interactive, with a strong emphasis on “experiential learning” through role/real play and ongoing feedback. The lab is led by certified skills-based lab instructors, who serve as facilitators in the process of student learning and practice.
