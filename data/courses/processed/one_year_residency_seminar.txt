TITLE: One-Year Residency Seminar
SUBJECT: Social Work
INSTRUCTOR: Allison B Berger
DESCRIPTION: Focusing on the body of knowledge, skills, and values that underpin and parallel course work, this seminar addresses the particular needs of the Reduced Residency student by integrating course concepts with field experience, emphasizing the socialization of the student toward the profession, and increasing their understanding of the societal and organizational contexts of the work. The format encourages learning flexibility, maximum student participation, and student support system development.
