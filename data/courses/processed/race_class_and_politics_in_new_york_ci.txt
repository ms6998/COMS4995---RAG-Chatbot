TITLE: RACE, CLASS, AND POLITICS IN NEW YORK CI
SUBJECT: History
INSTRUCTOR: Matthew Vaz
DESCRIPTION: The objectives of this course are: to gain familiarity with the major themes of New York History since 1898, to learn to think historically, and to learn to think and write critically about arguments that underlie historical interpretation. We will also examine and analyze the systems and structures--of race and class--that have shaped life in New York, while seeking to understand how social groups have pursued change inside and outside of such structures
