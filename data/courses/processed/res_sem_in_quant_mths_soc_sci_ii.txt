TITLE: RES SEM IN QUANT MTHS-SOC SCI II
SUBJECT: Quant Methods-social Sciences
INSTRUCTOR: Sandhya Kajeepeta
DESCRIPTION: This course has two goals.  One, it is designed to expose students in the QMSS degree program to different methods and practices of social science research.  Seminar presentations are given on a wide range of topics by faculty from Columbia and other New York City universities, as well as researchers from other settings. Two, it is also designed to give students important professional development skills, particularly around academic writing, research methods and job skills.
